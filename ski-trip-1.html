About Jackson Hole

Majestically nestled beneath the Grand Tetons, Jackson Hole is by far the steepest resort I've been to, with no shortage of glades and hidden chutes as not to mention the miles of hikeable terrain. Each year, CU Freeride does a trip for about 100 students, and last year I was lucky enough to make it.
